# **TechMaid**
## About **TechMaid**
TechMaid is an organization created by a guy based on his Highschool Company (techmaid Bistro), a company that serves drinks, pastry, pasta, and provide service such letting customer play a library of games as they enjoy their food order.<br>
<br>
This organization aims to be a indie game developing organization and will be releasing games under the name of TechMaid Games ([itch.io](https://techmaidgames.itch.io/)), pushing and pulling contents that is somewhat related to creating games, though may not be on par with big titles, or long-running developers, the organization aims to do this for the love of the game and aims to provide a genuine content.<br>
<br>
This TechMaid is created with the permission of the co-owner of the original company, and any decision made by this organization is completely on its own and not affected by the other co-owners or under the name of the original company. Each companies decisions will remain independent from one another.<br>
